Common Questions

Is it legal to host a casino night? +

Absolutely. All In Party Rentals events are strictly for entertainment — no real money is ever wagered or exchanged. Guests play with fun chips and compete for prizes, making our casino nights 100% legal across Nebraska. We've hosted hundreds of events and operate in full compliance with state regulations.

Can guests of all ages participate? +

Yes. Because no actual currency changes hands, our casino events are open to guests of all ages — making them a great fit for family reunions, school fundraisers, and mixed-age celebrations. If alcohol will be served at your event, just ensure your venue independently follows local liquor laws for age restrictions.

What's included in a rental? +

We provide everything needed for a complete casino experience: tables, cards, chips, dice, trays, and professional dealers. We handle full setup before your event and breakdown after — so your venue is left exactly as we found it. Custom casino-style chip sets are also available upon request for a personalized touch.

What types of events do you service? +

All of them. We've done corporate casino nights in Omaha, charity casino fundraisers for Nebraska nonprofits, birthday bashes, anniversary celebrations, bachelor and bachelorette parties, school events, and everything in between. Our packages are fully customizable — whether you're hosting 20 people in your living room or 500 in a ballroom, we scale to fit.

How do I book and what's the deposit? +

Booking is easy — reach out via the form below or give us a call and we'll check your date's availability. A 10% deposit secures your event on our calendar, with the remaining balance due on or before your event day. We recommend booking early, especially for Friday and Saturday evenings and peak seasons.

We primarily serve the greater Omaha metropolitan area — including Elkhorn, Gretna, Blair, Fremont, Bellevue, Plattsmouth, Nebraska City, Council Bluffs, Glenwood, Ashland, and Lincoln, Nebraska. We also travel to Kearney, Nebraska and the Kansas City area. Have an event outside our usual range? Contact us — we're happy to discuss options for events further afield.

Can I customize my package? +

Completely. From the mix of game tables to branded chip sets, themed décor, and dealer count — every detail of your event can be tailored to your vision and budget. During your initial consultation, we'll build a package around exactly what you need. Nothing cookie-cutter, ever.
